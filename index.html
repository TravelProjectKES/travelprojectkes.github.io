About Algarve

The Algarve is a stunning region in southern Portugal known for its beautiful beaches, picturesque coastal cliffs, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, historical sites, and outdoor activities.

Activities in Algarve

Sightseeing and Archaeology (Mr. John Savloy)

Explore historic sites such as the Roman Ruins of Milreu, Castle of Silves, and the ancient city of Faro.

Cycling, Swimming, and Spas (Mrs. Sarah Savloy)

Enjoy cycling along scenic coastal routes, visit stunning beaches like Praia da Marinha, and indulge in spa treatments at luxury resorts.

Rock Climbing and White Water Rafting (Fred)

Visit the Rocha da Pena Natural Park, which offers rock climbing opportunities, and try white water rafting in the Arade River.

Scuba Diving and Windsurfing (Joanna)

Explore the underwater world through scuba diving trips along the Algarve coast and experience thrilling windsurfing lessons in Lagos or Sagres.
